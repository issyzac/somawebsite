We are Somaapps

Somaapps Technologies Company Limited was created as a solution-centric company with the mission: "To accelerate the advent of mobile software technologies, artificial intelligence and the Internet of things." Today, we are researching and leveraging cutting-edge technologies in mobile software, AI and IoT to solve problems faced by people all over Africa. Technology runs through our blood and we love developing simple solutions to complex problems. We are committed to level the playing field by equipping the next generation of Africans with the necessary tools and knowledge required to build a better future for our home. We are Somaapps. This is our journey.

Focus on the Health and Future!

SomaFiti is a revolutionary device that tracks your every movement. Track your pulse rate in real time while working out and on your evening walk about town, every single step of the way.
